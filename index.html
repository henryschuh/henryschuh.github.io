About Me

I currently work at Google. Previously, I received my Ph.D. from the UW Paul G. Allen School, where I was advised by Prof. Arvind Krishnamurthy and Prof. Tom Anderson. I also worked concurrently with the SystemsResearch@Google group. My Ph.D. research focused on accelerating networked systems with emerging hardware technologies, in particular, programmable NICs and emerging coherent interconnects. Earlier, I was an undergraduate at Johns Hopkins University, where I studied Computer Science with a double major in Electrical Engineering.
